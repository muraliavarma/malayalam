# Malayalam poster notes

The big reference poster the user shared mixes vowels (സ്വരം) and consonants (വ്യഞ്ജനം). Below is the list I jotted down while zooming into the picture. Most entries are a direct reading of the Malayalam text that appears under each picture. For the rare sounds (ങ, ഛ, ഝ, ഠ, ഢ, ണ, ഥ, ള, ക്ഷ) the printing on the poster is fuzzy, so I copied the usual classroom word that matches the artwork (gong, umbrella, bells, procession staffs, drums, anklets, temple etc.). Please tweak any spelling you want in `src/data/cards.ts`.
